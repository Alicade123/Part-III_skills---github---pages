## 🚀 Introduction

Choosing the right automation tool is critical.

Here’s a practical comparison between **Playwright** and **Selenium**.

---

## ⚡ Speed & Performance

- Playwright: Faster, modern architecture  
- Selenium: Stable but slower  

---

## 🔧 Setup

- Playwright: Simple and developer-friendly  
- Selenium: Requires more configuration  

---

## 🧠 Use Case

- Playwright → Modern apps  
- Selenium → Legacy systems  

---

## 🔥 Verdict

If starting today, I recommend **Playwright** for most projects.
